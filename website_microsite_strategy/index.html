Project Brief & Strategic Report

Eskayvie Digital Campaign Ecosystem

Aligning product campaigns, microsite opportunities, SEO ownership, paid traffic routing and conversion flow between Eskayvie.com and product campaign assets.

Master Brand Eskayvie
Main Website eskayvie.com
Existing Microsite mindtropic.com.my
Potential Microsites Phytax & Diffenz

1. Executive Summary

Eskayvie currently operates with two important digital assets: Eskayvie.com as the main brand and ecommerce website, and Mindtropic.com.my as a product-specific microsite for Mindtropic direct conversion campaigns.

Mindtropic.com.my was created to support focused campaign storytelling, education, parent awareness and direct conversion. The traffic for this campaign comes from Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads and Google Ads.

Main issue Mindtropic.com.my is performing well and has started to outrank Eskayvie.com for Mindtropic-related keywords. This proves that the microsite works, but it also creates a risk where Eskayvie.com may lose SEO ownership and product authority for its own product.

At the same time, TTMM created wider traction beyond Mindtropic, especially for Diffenz and Phytax. The team has started Google Search Ads for these two products and is currently directing traffic to Eskayvie.com.

Updated opportunity There is also potential to create dedicated microsites for Phytax and Diffenz. This can become a growth opportunity, but it must be managed with a clear decision rule so Eskayvie does not create too many disconnected product domains.

The recommendation is not to reject microsites completely. Instead, Eskayvie should use a controlled model: Eskayvie.com remains the master brand and ecommerce hub, while product microsites can be created only when the business case, campaign size, tracking setup and SEO governance are clear.

4. What Happened with Mindtropic

Mindtropic.com.my was created with a clear purpose: to support direct conversion. It is more focused than a general product page because the full page can be built around one product, one audience and one conversion journey.

Key finding Mindtropic.com.my has started to outrank Eskayvie.com for Mindtropic-related keywords. This means the microsite is doing its job, but it also shows a bigger ownership issue.
What is working
  • Focused message for parents
  • Cleaner campaign storytelling
  • Better education flow for Mindtropic
  • Supports direct conversion from paid ads
  • Has SEO visibility and campaign momentum
What is risky
  • Eskayvie.com may lose product authority
  • Search traffic may sit outside the master brand
  • Brand ownership can become less clear
  • Tracking and reporting may become fragmented
  • The same issue may repeat with other products
Mindtropic.com.my is not failing. It is performing. The real question is how Eskayvie controls SEO ownership while using microsites for conversion.

This wider traction created opportunity for other products, especially Diffenz and Phytax. This means Eskayvie now needs a more structured marketing operation flow for multi-product campaigns.

6. Current Direction for Diffenz and Phytax

Unlike Mindtropic, the team is not using separate microsites for Diffenz and Phytax at this stage. Instead, Google Search Ads traffic is being directed to Eskayvie.com.

Why this is a strong current direction Sending Diffenz and Phytax Google Search Ads traffic to Eskayvie.com helps strengthen the main website as the product, ecommerce and conversion hub. It also reduces the risk of creating more SEO fragmentation too early.
  • It keeps product ownership under Eskayvie.
  • It helps build traffic and engagement for Eskayvie.com.
  • It keeps the customer journey closer to the official ecommerce flow.
  • It avoids creating too many separate websites too early.
  • It creates a cleaner reporting and tracking structure.

7. New Opportunity: Potential Microsites for Phytax and Diffenz

There is potential to create dedicated microsites for Phytax and Diffenz in the future. This should be viewed as an opportunity, not a problem. However, the Mindtropic experience shows that microsites must be controlled properly.

Strategic opportunity If Phytax and Diffenz require deeper product education, stronger storytelling, specific customer journeys or separate campaign positioning, a microsite can help improve direct conversion.
Why microsites can help
  • More focused product education
  • Cleaner conversion journey
  • Better landing page control for campaigns
  • Stronger storytelling around one product problem
  • Easier to test headline, offer, funnel and CTA
Why microsites must be controlled
  • Can compete with Eskayvie.com in search
  • Can split SEO authority between domains
  • Can confuse customers about official ownership
  • Can make reporting harder
  • Can increase maintenance workload
The opportunity is not to create more websites. The opportunity is to create better product funnels with clear ownership.

8. Key Issue: SEO Ownership and Marketing Operation Flow

The issue is not that Mindtropic.com.my is failing. The issue is that it is performing so well that it may create a separation between product visibility and master brand ownership.

Core strategic issue Mindtropic.com.my is winning visibility, but Eskayvie.com may be losing authority for one of its own products.

The bigger question is how Eskayvie should manage every product campaign moving forward. Without a clear rule, each product may start to build its own landing page, microsite, tracking setup and conversion path. This can make the whole ecosystem harder to manage.

12. Recommended Marketing Operation Flow

This is the proposed flow for future product campaigns, including situations where a microsite is being considered.

1. Campaign Trigger TTMM, ads, social content, KOL, PR or offline activation.
2. Product Interest Identify which product is gaining traction.
3. Funnel Need Decide whether product needs simple page or deep education funnel.
4. Destination Use Eskayvie.com first, or microsite if business case is strong.
5. Conversion Add to cart, WhatsApp lead, marketplace or official checkout.
6. Reporting Track by product, campaign, channel, keyword and conversion.

13. Decision Rule Before Creating Any New Product Microsite

A separate product microsite should not be created automatically. It should only be considered when there is a strong business reason.

Use Eskayvie.com
  • For evergreen product information
  • For Google Search Ads starting point
  • For ecommerce conversion
  • For long-term SEO ownership
Use Campaign Page
  • For promo or seasonal campaign
  • For specific audience angle
  • For funnel testing
  • Still under Eskayvie.com
Use Microsite If
  • Campaign is long-term
  • Product needs deep education
  • Product has strong standalone demand
  • Tracking and SEO governance are clear

Microsite Approval Checklist

Question Why It Matters
Does the product need a deeper education funnel? If yes, a microsite may help explain the problem, solution and proof better than a normal product page.
Is there enough paid media budget to drive traffic? A microsite should not be created without a clear traffic plan.
Is there enough search demand or campaign demand? Microsites need a clear audience and demand source to justify maintenance.
Can we maintain content, tracking and compliance? Every microsite adds operational workload.
How will the microsite support Eskayvie.com? The microsite must not weaken master brand ownership.

17. Management Decisions Required

  • Confirm Eskayvie.com as the main brand, product, ecommerce and SEO authority hub.
  • Confirm Mindtropic.com.my as an existing campaign and conversion microsite, not a disconnected product brand.
  • Confirm that Phytax and Diffenz have potential for microsites, but only after clear business, funnel and tracking evaluation.
  • Decide that new product campaigns should use Eskayvie.com first unless there is a strong reason for a separate microsite.
  • Approve a standard traffic routing model for paid ads and organic search.
  • Approve one reporting framework across products, channels, websites and conversion actions.

18. Final Recommendation

Recommended strategy Keep Mindtropic.com.my because it already has campaign and SEO momentum. However, its role must be clearly defined as a Mindtropic campaign and conversion microsite. Eskayvie.com should remain the main brand hub, ecommerce hub, official product ownership hub and long-term SEO authority.

For Diffenz and Phytax, the current move to send Google Search Ads traffic to Eskayvie.com is a good starting point. It helps strengthen the master brand ecosystem and avoids repeating the same SEO ownership issue too early.

However, Eskayvie should not close the door on future microsites for Phytax and Diffenz. If the products gain strong campaign traction, need deeper education, and have clear conversion goals, dedicated microsites can be developed under a controlled framework.

The key is not microsite versus Eskayvie.com. The key is control. Eskayvie needs high-converting product funnels without losing master brand ownership.
